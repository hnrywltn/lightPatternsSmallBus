Our story

A neighborhood
table since 2017.

We started Pepper & Salt in a 400 sq ft space with six tables and a used espresso machine. Eight years later we've moved to a bigger spot — but the food hasn't changed.

Everything is made from scratch. We source from farms within 60 miles when the season allows. And yes, the coffee is really that good.
